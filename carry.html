Methodology: Equity carry = forward div yield + buyback yield − financing cost (SOFR proxy); ETF yields from declared TTM distributions. FX carry = long currency rate − short currency rate (3M interbank). Fixed Income = YTM − financing. Commodity = −roll yield (structural estimate blended with observed ETF-spot basis, winsorized), annualized; positive = backwardation. Cross-class normalized via z-score within class. Dislocation z = carry vs the asset's own trailing history. Unwind fragility = carry richness + realized vol + extension, scaled by a live risk-regime multiplier.
