Methodology

Half-life = smallest horizon (in days) at which the signal's edge (hit_rate − 0.5) drops below 50% of its peak edge.
Edge status: FRESH stable, STRENGTHENING Δ90d > +10%, DECAYING Δ90d < −10%, DECAYED decaying AND peak edge < 2%, MARGINAL peak edge < 2%.
Recommended allocator weight: clamped to [0,1], scaled at 5× peak_edge.
