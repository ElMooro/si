⚖️ Capture Gap — Value Creation vs Value Capture

A company can be the single point of failure for its entire industry and still hold only a sliver of that industry's market cap. capture_gap = criticality percentile − market-cap-share percentile, computed within industry against the full listed market. Positive means the market underpays for indispensability. Criticality itself comes from margin level, margin stability (the fingerprint of pricing power), ROIC, R&D intensity and supply-chain centrality — not from the multiple.
