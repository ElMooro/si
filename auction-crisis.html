Methodology

How the v2 pipeline produces composite + 7 analytical layers + AI narrative

Data sources. Live: api.fiscaldata.treasury.gov (auction results), treasurydirect.gov (upcoming calendar), FRED (SOFR, IORB, DTWEXBGS, T10Y2Y, T5YIFR, DFF). Refresh: hourly via EventBridge.

1. Zero-rate bill floor. Diagnostic only when policy rate > 0. Bills auctioned at ≤ 0.001% with Fed funds > 1% = panic money parking. Saw this Sep 17-23, 2008 and Mar 19-26, 2020.

2. Bid-to-cover extremes. Z-scored vs 2024 baselines. High BTC on bills (≥+3σ) = flight-to-safety; low BTC on coupons (≤-2.5σ) = failed-auction warning.

3. Allotted-at-high tail stress. AAH > 95% on coupons = dealers absorbed the tail (Oct 9, 2024 had 99.31%); AAH < 15% on bills during stress = panic clustering.

4. Primary dealer absorption. PD share > 35% on coupons = dealers stuck (Sep 18, 2008 hit 69% — extreme); > 50% = GFC-tier.

5. Indirect (foreign) collapse. Foreign CB bid via NY Fed. < 50% on coupons = exodus pattern (Sep 18, 2008 hit 29%); 2024 baseline 60-78%.

6. Bill issuance explosion. 4-week vs 1-year average. > +30% = fiscal panic or liquidity injection. Overlay adds up to 15 pts to composite.

Tenor decomposition. Per-bucket (bills_lt_90d, bills_gte_90d, coupons_lt_3y, coupons_gt_3y, tips, frn) composite + rank + dominant signal. Identifies WHERE stress concentrates.

Forward calendar. Next 30 days pulled from TreasuryDirect. Per-auction forecast = 50% own-tenor stress + 25% cross-tenor contagion + 25% size shock, scaled by time-decay (1.0 ≤3d, 0.55 21-30d).

Historical analog matching. Current crisis vector (6D: zero_rate, btc, tail, pd, indirect, issuance) cosine-similarity vs each of 9 anchor crises. Top match + "what happened next" provides forward implication.

Tail risk probabilities. Heuristic models for P(failed auction in 30d), P(regime escalation in 14d), P(supply volatility in 30d). Driven by tenor stress + cross-signals + momentum.

AI commentary layer. Separate Lambda (justhodl-auction-crisis-ai) reads the full v2 data snapshot and calls claude-haiku-4-5-20251001 with a structured prompt. Produces narrative + per-upcoming-auction predictions + decisive call. 10-min cron offset from main data Lambda.

Composite formula: 70% × max-indicator + 30% × avg-indicator, weighted by accepted size over trailing 14 days. Issuance-explosion adds up to 15 points overlay.
