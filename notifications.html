Install the app and enable push notifications to receive compound-signal alerts, regime-change calls, EXIT directives, and nobrainer Tier-A picks directly on your phone or desktop — no Telegram, no email, no waiting. Works offline (cached shell).

iOS install: Tap the Share icon ↑ in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen". Push notifications require iOS 16.4+ and require the app to be installed first.

What you'll receive

Push notifications fire on these events (mirrors the Telegram bot but on your device directly):

  • Compound score crosses 200/300: exceptional stacking events
  • New Tier-A nobrainer: 9-dim convergence on a ticker
  • Regime change: SLOWING → CONTRACTION etc. (state transition)
  • EXIT directive: sizer flips to EXIT_ALL_RISK
  • Insider cluster: 3+ insider buys ≥$100k in 30d on a single ticker
  • Eurodollar stress score crosses 70: liquidity warning

Compatibility

How it works: Browser-native Web Push API (W3C standard). Subscriptions stored in justhodl-push-subscriptions DynamoDB table. Server signs with VAPID keys (P-256 ECDSA), delivers via FCM/APNs/etc. through the browser's push service. Encrypted end-to-end.
