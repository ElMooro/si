Why this matters — eurodollar lens
Cross-border bank claims are the balance-sheet footprint of the offshore dollar system. Accelerating claims on offshore centres (Cayman, Hong Kong, Singapore, Luxembourg) = the eurodollar machine expanding — risk-asset supportive but fragility-building. Deceleration or contraction historically precedes global dollar squeezes. Pair with the Eurodollar Plumbing board (funding rates, dealer positioning, FX basis) for the flow-vs-stock picture.
