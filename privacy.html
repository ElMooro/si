Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JustHodl.AI ("JustHodl", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website (justhodl.ai), desktop app, and mobile apps (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to this policy.

The short version. We collect the minimum needed to run the Service. We do not sell your personal data. We do not show third-party ads. If you create an account, we store your email and your in-app preferences (watchlists, settings). If you opt into mobile alerts, we store a push token solely to deliver those alerts.

1. Information we collect

a) Information you provide

  • Account information (optional) — if you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google or another provider (OAuth), the basic profile information that provider shares (name, email). Authentication is handled by our provider, Supabase.
  • Payment information (if you subscribe) — processed by our payment provider, Stripe. We do not store your full card number; Stripe does. We receive only subscription status and the last four digits / card brand.
  • Preferences you set — watchlists, alerts, chart settings, and similar in-app configuration you choose to save.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — basic technical data such as device type, OS version, app version, and crash/diagnostic logs, used to keep the app working.
  • Push notification token (mobile, optional) — if you enable alerts, your device's push token is stored so we can deliver the alerts you opted into. You can disable this anytime in your device settings.
  • Local storage — the app stores settings on your device; some preferences sync to our servers if you are signed in.
What we do NOT collect: we do not collect your contacts, photos, precise location, microphone, or browsing history outside the Service. We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising.

We process this data to provide and improve the Service, secure it against abuse, and comply with law. We do not use your personal data to train third-party advertising models, and we do not sell it.

3. Market data

The Service displays financial and market data sourced from third-party providers (including FRED/Federal Reserve, and market-data vendors). This is public/market information about securities and markets — it is not your personal information.

4. Service providers we share with

We share limited data with infrastructure providers strictly to operate the Service:

  • Supabase — authentication & account database
  • Stripe — payment processing (subscriptions)
  • Amazon Web Services & Cloudflare — hosting, storage, and content delivery
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — to deliver mobile push alerts you opt into

These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments. We do not sell data to anyone.

5. Data retention

We keep account data while your account is active. Push tokens expire automatically (about 90 days) and refresh when you open the app. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Contact).

6. Your choices & rights

  • Access / deletion — request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing us.
  • Alerts — turn push notifications on/off in your device settings.
  • Account — you can use much of the Service without an account; sign out or delete your account anytime.
  • Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR/EEA, CCPA/California), you may have additional rights to access, correct, port, or delete your data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls, reputable infrastructure providers) to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

8. Children

The Service is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International users

The Service is operated from the United States. By using it, you understand your information may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where our providers operate.

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, in-app notice.
