SHORT VOLUME RATIO (SVR)
SVR = ShortVolume / TotalVolume  ·  % of today's trading executed short
Source: FINRA CNMS Reg SHO Daily file — consolidated across all venues
T+1 publication, ~7:45 PM ET
Z-SCORE (60-day distribution)
z = (SVR_today − mean_60d) / stdev_60d
z > 2 = abnormal · z > 2.5 = extreme · z > 3 = once in 100 days
MOMENTUM
momentum = SVR_5d_avg − SVR_20d_avg  ·  Positive = building short pressure
DAYS-TO-COVER (20-day cumulative basis)
DTC = Σ short_vol_20d / avg_total_vol_20d
Forced-cover risk: how many trading days at current volume to fully cover
