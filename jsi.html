Methodology. Spine components (VIX · Chicago-Fed NFCI · KC-Fed KCFSI · St-Louis FSI · high-yield & IG credit OAS · 10Y-2Y yield curve) are each z-scored on their full history since 1990, logistic-mapped to a 0-100 stress sub-score, and weighted-blended. The identical computation runs across every era, so the percentile-since-1990 is a true continuous statement rather than a spliced series. The live overlay of JustHodl stress engines is contemporary enrichment only and never reaches into history. Present score = 0.70·spine + 0.30·overlay; historical percentile is always measured against the pure-spine series.
Data: jsi.json · jsi-history.json · Refresh: every 6h via justhodl-stress-index · Sources: FRED (spine, since 1990) + JustHodl stress fleet (overlay).
