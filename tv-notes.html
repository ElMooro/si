TradingView Notes → Brain

Harvest every text note you've written under any ticker on TradingView and load them straight into your Brain — from your own logged-in browser, no credentials shared.
Notes already harvested into the mirror: …
1
Open TradingView in the same Chrome where you're logged in. Open a chart, then the Notes widget (right-side panel, or press Alt+N) and click All notes at the top-right of that widget.
2
Press F12 → Console tab. Paste the line below and hit Enter. A small amber panel appears bottom-right of TradingView. 
javascript:(function(){var s=document.createElement('script');s.src='https://justhodl.ai/tools/tv-export.js?t='+Date.now();document.body.appendChild(s);})();
Chrome may block the first paste in console with a "warning" — type allow pasting once, press Enter, then paste again.
3
In the All notes list, click through each symbol that has a note. The panel counts them as they're captured — no copy-paste needed. When the count stops climbing, press UPLOAD.
4
Done. Your notes are in the Brain, tagged [TV:SYMBOL], deduped, and visible at brain.html. The next brain-compiler run routes them to the engines that can use them.

Everything runs inside your own browser session on tradingview.com. The extractor only reads your notes and sends them to your own JustHodl ingest endpoint — nothing else leaves the page, and nothing sends until you press UPLOAD. TradingView has no official notes API, so this rides your live session; if TV changes their internal note API, the capture may need a refresh.
