1
Press Ctrl+Shift+O in Chrome to open Bookmark Manager.
In the left panel, right-click Bookmarks bar → Add new bookmark.
2
Name: type JH TV Notes
URL: click inside the box below (it auto-selects all), then Ctrl+C to copy, and paste it as the bookmark URL. Click Save.

⚠ The URL starts with javascript: — that's correct. It's a bookmarklet, not a website address.
3
Go to tradingview.com (logged in) and click "JH TV Notes" in your bookmarks bar.
An amber panel appears bottom-right. It automatically tries TV's internal notes API. Also open the Notes widget → All Notes and click through each symbol — every note that loads is captured. When the count stops climbing, press UPLOAD TO BRAIN.
If the bookmarks bar isn't visible
Show it: Ctrl+Shift+B in Chrome. Or right-click the Chrome toolbar and enable Show bookmarks bar.
Everything runs inside your own browser. The bookmarklet is self-contained — all JavaScript inline, no external scripts loaded. Nothing leaves until you press UPLOAD. Notes are tagged [TV:SYMBOL] and deduplicated. Brain-compiler routes them to every matching engine on its next run.
