AI Methodology: The AI Flow Strategist runs daily after the flow engine. It ingests today's 84-ETF flow data, all current equity research verdicts, EDGAR insider signals, crisis-KB regime tag, and 30-day flow history, then generates structured output via Claude Sonnet 4.6 (1h cached system prompt). Each ticker call cites signal_alignment — exactly which feeds support it. 4+ aligned = HIGH conviction; 1 isolated signal = LOW. The model is instructed to use only data in the input (no hallucination) and to assign explicit position sizes + stop levels on every call.

Flow Methodology: Daily flows = share creations × NAV − share redemptions × NAV (sourced from Polygon ETF Global, processed_date = previous trading day). Z-score: today's daily flow vs trailing 90-day distribution. Composite signals normalize the spread between bucket averages (e.g., defensive minus cyclical avg z-scores) through a ±4σ clamp. Refreshes daily 17:00 ET (flows) and 17:30 ET (AI note). Sources: etf-flows/daily.json, etf-flows/ai-analysis.json. Academic backing: Ben-David 2017, BIS 2018 — ETF flows are a leading indicator of underlying price action.
