Methodology: Daily flows = sum of share creations × NAV − sum of share redemptions × NAV (sourced from Polygon ETF Global). Z-score: today's daily flow vs trailing 90-day distribution. Labels: |z|≥2.0 → STRONG_INFLOW/OUTFLOW, |z|≥1.0 → INFLOW/OUTFLOW, else NEUTRAL. Smart money = institutional-favored ETFs (SPY/QQQ/sector SPDRs). Dumb money = retail-favored (ARKK/SOXL/leveraged/single-theme). Composite scores normalize to ±100 by mapping the smart−dumb (or defensive−cyclical) z-score spread through a ±4σ clamp. Regime classifier: DEFENSIVE if defensive_rotation≥+30 AND risk_on_off≤−30; RISK_ON if both ≥+30; CREDIT_STRESS if credit_stress≥+40; else TRANSITION/NEUTRAL. Refreshes daily 17:00 ET (after Polygon EOD processing). Source: etf-flows/daily.json, etf-flows/composite.json. Academic backing: Ben-David 2017, BIS 2018, Israeli 2017 — ETF flows are a leading indicator of underlying price action.
