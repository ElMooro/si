About JustHodl.AI

JustHodl.AI is an institutional-grade financial-intelligence terminal that turns the data hedge funds pay for into something you can actually use. It fuses dozens of independent signal engines — relative-value dislocations, quality-growth compounders, institutional capital flow, congressional & insider activity, options flow, and a cross-asset macro/bond-vol regime model — into one ranked conviction view, explained in plain language.

What makes it different

  • Full universe, all caps — every signal runs across ~1,800 names, micro to mega.
  • Confluence over noise — the strongest setups are where multiple independent lenses agree (our "Triple" and "Quad Threat" signals).
  • Self-grading — a live, public track record measures what actually worked, including the losers.
  • Regime-aware — conviction is tempered automatically when bond-vol signals risk-off.
  • Explainable — every signal carries a data-cited "why," not a black box.

Who it's for

Self-directed investors, swing and macro traders, and analysts who want an edge without a Bloomberg terminal. It is research and analytics — a tool to inform your own decisions.
