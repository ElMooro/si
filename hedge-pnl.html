💰 Hedge Overlay Scorecard

Is the tail-hedge overlay earning its carry? A tail hedge is insurance — it is meant to bleed a small carry between events and pay convexly in a stress episode, so the test is cumulative convex payoff against cumulative carry, not whether it is green.
