Methodology

For each scored outcome where correct ∈ {True, False}, the engine computes: contribution = 0.02 × weight × predicted_direction_sign × actual_return. Each outcome is treated as a hypothetical 2%-of-NAV trade modulated by the signal's calibration weight — so a w=1.5 signal earning +10% contributes 0.30% to NAV. Daily contributions sum and NAV compounds normally from $100,000. This is a what-if estimator: if you had sized every realized signal by its current calibration weight at 2% per trade, this is the alpha curve you would have produced. Compare the final return % to SPY buy-and-hold over the same window. Auto-recomputes every 6 hours after the calibrator runs.
