Sizing Engine — signal → size

Every live directional signal arrives with a position size built from measured edge: quarter-Kelly per engine from the closed loop's own graded record, confidence deflated by the calibrator's self-audit, a 30% annualized vol budget, and a cluster-correlation haircut. Prudence floors: losses assumed ≥0.75% (cost+slippage), payoff capped 6×, Kelly capped 30% — engines with near-zero historical losses get an *. Engines with negative measured Kelly land on the fade list and are never sized. Sizes are per $100k of book — research, not advice.
