🎯 Signal Scorecard

Every signal the platform tracks, graded on its realised edge. A signal is scored only on outcomes with a directional prediction and a resolved realised move — neutral predictions, pre-baseline legacy records and unpriceable outcomes are reported separately, never counted as misses. Hit rates use a Wilson 95% lower bound, so a small sample earns no credit until it proves itself. Signals that prove (n≥25) they cannot beat a coin flip are DEPRECATED and their weight is set to zero. Quality over quantity, enforced on evidence.
