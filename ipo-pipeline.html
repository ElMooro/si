IPO Pipeline Methodology

Upcoming window: next 60 calendar days of scheduled IPOs from FMP's /stable/ipos-calendar feed. Includes ticker, exchange, expected pricing date, price range, and shares offered (when disclosed).

Recent window: IPOs priced in the last 90 calendar days. For each, current price is fetched via /stable/quote and return-since-IPO is computed against the midpoint of the offering range (or the offer price if disclosed). Returns of +positive indicate trading above offering price.

Regime bands: IPO_BOOM avg ≥+25% · IPO_HEALTHY +5…+25% · IPO_MIXED −10…+5% · IPO_BUST ≤−10%. Boom regimes historically precede top-of-cycle (1999, 2007, 2021); bust regimes precede cleansing (2002, 2009, 2022) and tactical re-entry windows.

Edge basis: Ritter & Welch 2002 documented average first-day pop of 17.9%; Loughran & Ritter 1995 showed 3-year post-IPO underperformance of ~22% vs market. Bloomberg's IPO function + Refinitiv's ECM module are the two most-used allocator tools for ECM monitoring.
