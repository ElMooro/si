Who is really returning capital — and who is quietly printing shares. Loading…
How to read this desk. The ground truth of dilution is the diluted share count — if it grows, every headline buyback is losing the race. Net buyback = TTM repurchases minus TTM new issuance from the cash-flow statement; a company can announce billions in buybacks that only mop up stock-comp issuance — we flag that SBC WASH. Insider money is joined from the Form-4 desks: open-market purchases are real conviction with management's own cash; the sell side uses the 5-factor opportunistic-sale filter (routine 10b5-1 noise removed). The nastiest pattern on Wall Street — the company buying back stock with shareholder cash while management sells its own — has its own board below. Research, not advice.
