🟢 System Uptime · Heartbeat Monitor

Catches silent failures. When Claude occasionally returns malformed JSON, a Lambda errors silently, an S3 write fails, or an EventBridge rule gets disabled, the platform stops producing fresh briefs without you noticing. This page is the heartbeat. An hourly cron checks the age of every critical artifact (both front-run sniffers, both digests, CIO synthesis, portfolio brief) against per-brief thresholds tuned to their expected refresh cadence. Telegram fires on any FRESH→STALE transition (priority alert), every 4h while persistently stale, and once on ALL_CLEAR when everything's healthy again.
