🌪 Volatility Risk Premium

Implied volatility minus realized volatility — what the options market charges to underwrite vol insurance, over and above the vol that actually occurs. RICH means selling vol is well paid (and complacency); THIN means the premium is compressed; INVERTED — realized above implied — is an early stress signal.
