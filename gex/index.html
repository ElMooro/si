GAMMA EXPOSURE (GEX)
GEX per contract = ±OI × 100 × spot² × γ × 0.01
+ for calls (dealers short gamma → buy on rise)  ·  - for puts
Units: dollars per 1% move in underlying
Total Dealer GEX = Σ across all strikes & expiries
ZERO-GAMMA FLIP LEVEL
Iterate spot ∈ [−5%, +5%] in 0.25% steps · find where cumulative GEX crosses zero
Above flip = positive gamma = dealers stabilize tape (mean revert)
Below flip = negative gamma = dealers amplify moves (momentum/explosive)
VANNA & CHARM
Vanna = ∂Δ/∂σ ≈ −d₂ × φ(d₁) / σ  ·  IV crush ⟹ delta change ⟹ hedging
Charm = ∂Δ/∂t  ·  time decay drives pinning toward max-pain at expiry
MAX PAIN (per expiry)
Strike K minimizing: Σ max(0, S−K) × call_OI + max(0, K−S) × put_OI
Gravitational pull toward this strike at expiry
IV SKEW (30-day)
IV_25-delta-put − IV_25-delta-call
Higher = expensive crash hedges = market stress
