How to read this — and what the scorecard means
This board surfaces stocks flagged by several independent alpha systems at once — convergence, not a single screen. Tap any row for full research: a plain-English thesis, the bear case, valuation on two axes (vs industry and vs the stock's own history), 10-year financials, quality & solvency, who else is positioned, and an honest bull/bear scorecard. The scorecard lists the alpha systems backing it on one side and the fundamental red flags on the other — the disagreement is the useful part. More systems isn't automatically better; a name flagged by 6 systems with 4 red flags is a different bet than 6 systems and none.
