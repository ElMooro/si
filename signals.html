Why aggregating these matters

Most analysts sit on one data source and trust their gut to weight signals. The real alpha is when independent sources converge: insider clusters at companies showing positive 8-K material events, AAII showing extreme bearishness when GDELT shows negative narrative saturation, OECD CLI ticking up while JOLTS layoffs decline. The combination is worth 10× any single source because each filters out the others' false positives.

This page shows you the latest reading from each source. The justhodl-asymmetric-scorer Lambda will be wired to read these next — turning the aggregate into a single conviction score per ticker.
